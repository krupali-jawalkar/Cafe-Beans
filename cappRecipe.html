What You'll Need

- 1 shot of espresso
- 100-120ml of milk
- Cocoa powder or cinnamon for dusting (optional)
HOW TO MAKE CAPPUCCINO
1.Brew a shot of espresso (25-30ml).
2.Froth 100-120ml of milk using a steam wand until it has a velvety texture and microfoam.
3. Pour the frothed milk over the espresso in a 1:1:1 ratio (espresso, milk, foam).
4. Optionally, dust the top with cocoa powder or cinnamon.
