What You'll Need

- 1 shot of espresso

- 150 to 180ml of milk
HOW TO MAKE ESPRESSO
1. Brew a shot of espresso (25-30ml).
Froth 150-180ml of milk using a steam wand until it has a creamy texture.
3. Pour the frothed milk over the espresso, holding back the foam with a spoon.
4. Spoon the foam over the top of the latte.
