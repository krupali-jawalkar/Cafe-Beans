What You'll Need

- 1 shot of espresso
- Hot water
HOW TO MAKE ESPRESSO
1. Grind 18-20 grams of coffee beans finely.
2. Place the ground coffee in a portafilter and tamp it down firmly.
3. Insert the portafilter into an espresso machine and brew for about 25-30 seconds.
4. Serve immediately in a small espresso cup.
