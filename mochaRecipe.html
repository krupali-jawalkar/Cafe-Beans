What You'll Need

- 1 shot of espresso
- 150ml of milk
- 2 tablespoons of chocolate syrup
- Whipped cream for topping
HOW TO MAKE ESPRESSO
1. Brew a shot of espresso (25-30ml).
2. Heat 150ml of milk and stir in 2 tablespoons of chocolate syrup until dissolved.
3. Pour the chocolate milk into the espresso.
4. Top with whipped cream and a drizzle of chocolate syrup.
