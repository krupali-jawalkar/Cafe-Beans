What You'll Need

- Hot coffee
- 1-2 teaspoons of brown sugar
- 45ml of Irish whiskey
- Whipped cream
- Nutmeg or cocoa powder for garnish (optional)
HOW TO MAKE CAPPUCCINO
1. Brew a cup of hot coffee.
2. Add 1-2 teaspoons of brown sugar to a glass or mug.
3. Pour in 45ml of Irish whiskey and stir until the sugar dissolves.
4. Top with hot coffee, leaving some space at the top.
5. Gently pour whipped cream over the back of a spoon to create a layer on top of the coffee.
6. Optionally, garnish with a sprinkle of nutmeg or cocoa powder.
