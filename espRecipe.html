What You'll Need

- Freshly ground coffee
- Espresso machine
- Filtered water
- Tamper
- Coffee scale
- Portafilter
- Demitasse cup
HOW TO MAKE ESPRESSO
1. Preheat Machine

Turn on the espresso machine and let it preheat.

2. Measure Coffee

Weigh 18-20 grams of coffee for a double shot.

3. Grind Coffee

Grind to a fine consistency.

4. Prepare Portafilter

Fill portafilter with coffee, distribute evenly.

5. Tamp Coffee

Tamp firmly to create a flat, even surface.

6. Insert Portafilter

Lock portafilter into the machine.

7. Brew Espresso

Place demitasse cup under spout and start the machine.

8. Serve

Once done, serve the espresso immediately.
